Akan Names.

About

The Akan people of Ghana ferquently name their children after the days of the week they were born in. These names have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. This naming tradition is shared all through West Africa. A few examples of people that had these names are Kwame Nkrumah, Kofi Annan and many others.
